What Lurks Beneath the Map?

Ever heard a chilling local legend about that old tree in your barangay, felt a shiver down your spine walking past a certain abandoned house, or had an experience you just had to tell someone who'd understand? What if all those whispered tales, local myths, and firsthand encounters from across the Philippines were gathered in ONE place, pinpointed exactly where they happened?

Eerie Grid PH is a community-driven platform dedicated to collecting and mapping these horror stories from across the Philippines. We believe that every town, every street, every old building holds a secret, a chilling tale waiting to be told.

Explore the Unknown:

Interactive Horror Map

Navigate an interactive map bleeding with stories. Discover haunted locales near you or explore the grim tales of distant islands. Each pin a heartbeat, each story a shiver.

Share Your Encounters

Become a part of the narrative. Pin your own paranormal experiences, local urban legends, or family ghost stories directly onto the map for others to read and feel the chill.

A Community of Fear

Read tales submitted by fellow horror enthusiasts. From ancient folklore to modern-day scares, connect with a community that shares your passion for the macabre and the unexplained.
